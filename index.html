Welcome, I'm tiredcheetah888! I am a lover of all things art and design. This fall, I dove into UI/UX design for the first time in my computer science class at Brown University. Click through my portfolio pieces below to see how I've grown as a designer this semester!

I. Personas & Storyboarding

I began my journey of exploring user design with this Personas & Storyboarding project. I learned how to empathize with users and create a storyboard based upon personas I created from observations of how they interacted with a select interface.

II. Responsive Redesign

I practiced the workflow of redesigning a website for a local Providence nail salon, Metro Nails, because it appeared outdated and had significant usability issues. It was great to design a solution for an establishment I reguarly visit!

III. Iterative Design

I worked in a team to design an app for a vintage wholesale marketplace start-up with feedback and user testing. I had a great time growing my prototyping skills and experiencing the design process as a team.

IV. Development

I got even more comfortable with React by creating a list-based interface! I developed an interactive interface, used interface components, and tied the components to an internal data state.
